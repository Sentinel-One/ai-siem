1. The problem 30s

Our SOC drowns in low-severity, individually-uninteresting signals: encoded PowerShell, a recon command, a LOLBin download. Alert on each and you get fatigue; ignore them and you miss the slow-burn attack. RBA fixes this: we don't alert on the observation, we accumulate risk on the entity behind it.

2. Contributors → risk index 45s · open Live risk tab

Each noisy-but-interesting behaviour is a contributor. Instead of an alert it publishes a low-noise risk event into a dedicated risk index, tagged with a base score and a MITRE tactic. None of these fire an alert on their own.

3. Risk factors from AD / ISPM 45s · open Editor tab

Risk isn't flat, it's amplified by who the entity is. The factor table is sourced from Active Directory via ISPM / Ranger AD: a privileged account is weighted ×2 automatically, a service account ×0.5. This is the SentinelOne equivalent of Splunk RBA importing assets from AD, sourced natively.

4. The money slide — amplification 60s · Live risk leaderboard

Watch adm.webb vs adm.kowalski: same four behaviours, but adm.webb is privileged, so ×2 puts them at 150 across 4 tactics; adm.kowalski is standard, ×1, at 55. svc-backup does similar things but ×0.5 keeps it at 17.5, below threshold and deliberately silent. Identical behaviour, different outcome, driven by AD context.

5. One high-fidelity alert 45s

When cumulative risk crosses a threshold, one alert fires, entity-bound, with the whole story attached. adm.webb and the host FIN-WKS-018 already generated HIGH alerts, each carrying its contributing timeline. The 7-day multi-tactic rule catches the low-and-slow campaign the 24h score misses.

6. Self-running and tunable 30s · Editor tab

The collector is a scheduled Hyperautomation flow: it runs the contributors, applies the AD multipliers, and publishes on a cadence, no analyst in the loop. And SYSTEM sitting near the top? That's the honest part: RBA is a system you cultivate. Real attacker behaviour rises; benign noise gets factored down. In the Editor tab I can drop SYSTEM to ×0.2 live.

Close 15s

Net: fewer, richer alerts; prioritised by business context from your own AD; and a reusable internal library of what "interesting" looks like in this environment.
